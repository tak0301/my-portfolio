LGBT Mental Health

Multimedia Production, Instructional Design

LGBT Mental Health is a course offered through The George Washington Universityâ€™s LGBT Health Policy certificate program. The course is offered each year to GW students participating in the certificate program.

As instructional designer for this course, some of my main roles included assisting the course instructor with course planning, platform (Blackboard) development and the incorporation of live synchronous sessions (Blackboard Collaborate) into course design. However, like many of the courses I work on, my responsibilities extended beyond instructional design into media production. I worked closely with the course instructor to envision a series of discussion-based videos that would not only be used to introduce students to important course topics, but also encourage class dialogue directly within the video module.
