Chikungunya Prevention Video

Multimedia Production

This was a special stand-alone video project designed in collaboration with The George Washington University’s School of Nursing. The video was developed to combat the increasing prevalence of Chikungunya, a mosquito-carrying virus, in Haiti. It was shown in November of 2015 by members of GW’s School of Nursing during an annual trip to Haiti.

As the lead producer for this project, I was responsible for overall project management and ensuring the successful and timely completion of the video. I also worked closely with a GW School of Nursing faculty member to write the video script, as well as recruit, select and manage the voice-over talent. Additionally, I collaborated closely with the animator to support overall video design and development.
